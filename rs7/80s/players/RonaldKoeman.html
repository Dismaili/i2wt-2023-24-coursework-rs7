    Ronald Koeman

    Short bio

    Ronald Koeman, born on March 21, 1963, in Zaandam, Netherlands, is a retired Dutch footballer and a highly successful football manager. Koeman was known for his skill as a free-kick specialist and for his ability to play as a central defender or defensive midfielder.

    After a distinguished playing career, Koeman transitioned into coaching and has achieved success managing top clubs and national teams. His tactical acumen and leadership qualities have made him a respected figure in the world of football.
