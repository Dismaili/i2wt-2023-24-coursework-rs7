    Franco Baresi

    Short bio

    Franco Baresi, born on May 8, 1960, in Travagliato, Italy, is a retired Italian footballer and one of the greatest defenders in the history of the sport. Known for his exceptional defensive skills, leadership, and loyalty, Baresi spent the entirety of his playing career with AC Milan, becoming a legendary figure in Italian football.

    Baresi's career began with AC Milan's youth system, and he quickly rose through the ranks to become the captain of the first team. His defensive prowess and ability to read the game earned him widespread acclaim, making him a cornerstone of AC Milan's success during the late '80s and early '90s.
