    Marco van Basten

    Short bio

    Marco van Basten, born on October 31, 1964, in Utrecht, Netherlands, is a retired Dutch footballer and one of the greatest strikers in the history of the sport. Van Basten's prolific goal-scoring ability, technical skill, and elegance on the field made him a football icon.

    Van Basten began his professional career with Ajax before moving to AC Milan, where he achieved unparalleled success. Unfortunately, his career was cut short due to persistent injuries, but his impact on the game remains indelible.
