    Bernd Schuster

    Short bio

    Bernd Schuster, born on December 22, 1959, in Augsburg, Germany, is a retired German footballer and a successful football manager. Known for his elegant style of play and exceptional vision on the field, Schuster made a significant impact during his playing career.

    Schuster's journey in football began with FC Augsburg, his hometown club, before he gained prominence at FC Köln. He played a crucial role in the midfield and caught the attention of top European clubs with his ball control and passing ability.
