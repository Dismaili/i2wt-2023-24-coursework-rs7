    Ruud Gullit

    Short bio

    Ruud Gullit, born on September 1, 1962, in Amsterdam, Netherlands, is a retired Dutch footballer and a prominent football manager. Gullit was known for his versatility, elegance, and leadership on the pitch, making him one of the most iconic players of his generation.

    After his playing career, Gullit transitioned into coaching and management, showcasing his tactical knowledge and passion for the game. His impact extends beyond the field, as he continues to contribute to the sport as a football pundit and ambassador.
