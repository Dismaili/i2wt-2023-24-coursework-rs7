    Alessandro Nesta

    Short bio

    Alessandro Nesta, born on March 19, 1976, in Rome, Italy, is a retired Italian footballer celebrated for his stellar career as a central defender. Rising through the ranks at Lazio, he played a key role in their Serie A triumph in 2000. Nesta later joined AC Milan, contributing to two Serie A and UEFA Champions League victories. Internationally, he played a crucial part in Italy's 2006 World Cup win. Recognized for his defensive prowess, Nesta received multiple Serie A Defender of the Year awards. Post-retirement, he transitioned to coaching, leaving an enduring legacy in Italian football.
