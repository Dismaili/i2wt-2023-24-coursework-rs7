    Fernando Hierro

    Short bio

    Fernando Hierro, born on March 23, 1968, in Vélez-Málaga, Spain, is a retired Spanish footballer and former coach. Renowned for his versatility, he played a significant part of his career at Real Madrid, winning multiple La Liga and UEFA Champions League titles. As a key figure in the Spanish national team, Hierro represented Spain in several World Cups and European Championships. Post-retirement, he ventured into coaching and briefly served as Spain's national team coach during the 2018 World Cup. Hierro's legacy is marked by his impact on both club and international levels in Spanish football.
