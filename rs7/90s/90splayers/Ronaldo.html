    Ronaldo

    Short bio

    Ronaldo Luís Nazário de Lima, born on September 22, 1976, in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, is a retired football legend celebrated for his incredible skill and goal-scoring prowess. His illustrious career included successful stints with clubs like Barcelona, Inter Milan, and Real Madrid. Ronaldo won two FIFA World Player of the Year awards and secured two World Cup titles with the Brazilian national team (1994, 2002). Despite battling career-threatening injuries, he made a remarkable comeback, leaving an indelible mark on the sport. Ronaldo's legacy is defined by his speed, clinical finishing, and contributions to both club and country.
