    Paolo Maldini

    Short bio

    Paolo Maldini, born on June 26, 1968, in Milan, Italy, is a football icon renowned for his exceptional career with A.C. Milan. Spending his entire 25-year playing career at the club, Maldini played a crucial role in securing seven Serie A titles and five UEFA Champions League trophies. Known for his defensive prowess and leadership, he captained A.C. Milan for many years. Maldini also represented Italy in multiple European Championships and World Cups. After retiring in 2009, he remains a symbol of loyalty and excellence in the football world. Post-retirement, Maldini has been actively involved in football administration, notably with A.C. Milan.
