    Roberto Baggio

    Short bio

    Roberto Baggio, born on February 18, 1967, in Caldogno, Italy, is a retired football icon celebrated for his elegance and skill. His illustrious career included stints with top Italian clubs like Juventus, Fiorentina, and Milan. Baggio's international success was highlighted by leading Italy to the 1994 World Cup final and winning individual awards like the FIFA World Player of the Year (1993) and the Ballon d'Or (1993). Post-retirement, he engaged in humanitarian work as a UNESCO Goodwill Ambassador, showcasing his impact both on and off the field. Baggio's legacy resonates as one of the greatest players in football history.
