    Roy Keane

    Short bio

    Roy Keane, born on August 10, 1971, in Cork, Ireland, is a retired Irish footballer known for his tenacity and leadership. His notable career includes a successful stint with Manchester United from 1993 to 2005, where he won multiple Premier League titles and the UEFA Champions League in 1999. Keane represented the Republic of Ireland internationally and later transitioned into management, taking charge of clubs like Sunderland. Post-retirement, he became a respected football pundit, contributing to the sport's analysis and commentary. Keane's legacy is defined by his fierce playing style, leadership qualities, and enduring impact on football.
