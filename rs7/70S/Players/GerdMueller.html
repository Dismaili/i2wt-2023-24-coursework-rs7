    Gerd Müller

    Short bio

    Gerhard "Gerd" Müller (November 3, 1945 - August 15, 2021) was a German professional football player. Known for his remarkable goal-scoring abilities, particularly in and around the six-yard box, he is widely recognized as one of the most accomplished goal-scorers in the history of the sport. Having achieved success both at the club and international levels, he stands among the select group of nine players who have secured victory in the FIFA World Cup, the UEFA Champions League, and the Ballon d'Or.
