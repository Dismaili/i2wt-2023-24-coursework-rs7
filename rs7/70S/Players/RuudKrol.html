    Ruud Krol

    Short bio

    Rudolf ("Ruud" or "Rudi") Jozef Krol (born March 24, 1949) is a retired Dutch professional footballer who earned 83 caps for the Netherlands national team. Primarily associated with his hometown club, Ajax, for the majority of his career, he later transitioned to coaching following his retirement. Widely regarded as one of the finest defenders in the history of the sport, Krol predominantly operated as a sweeper or left-back. However, his versatility allowed him to play anywhere along the defensive line or even in midfield as a defensive midfielder, owing to his adept passing with both feet, composed temperament, tactical acumen, and the ability to initiate attacking plays after regaining possession.
