    Berti Vogts

    Short bio

    Hans-Hubert "Berti" Vogts (born December 30, 1946) is a retired German professional footballer renowned as a defender. Throughout his entire club career, he represented Borussia Mönchengladbach in the Bundesliga. Vogts achieved the pinnacle of success by winning the FIFA World Cup with West Germany in 1974. Subsequently, he transitioned to a successful managerial career, overseeing national teams such as Germany (leading them to victory in Euro 96), Scotland, Nigeria, and Azerbaijan.
