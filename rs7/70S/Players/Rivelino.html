    Rivelino

    Short bio

    Roberto Rivellino (also known as Rivelino, born January 1, 1946) is a Brazilian football commentator and retired football player. He played a prominent role in Brazil's 1970 FIFA World Cup championship team. Rivellino is currently engaged as a pundit for Brazilian TV Cultura.
