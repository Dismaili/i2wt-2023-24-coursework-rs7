    Johan Neeskens

    Short bio

    Johannes Jacobus Neeskens (born September 15, 1951) is a Dutch football manager and former player. Playing as a midfielder, he played a crucial role in the Netherlands national team's runner-up finishes in the 1974 and 1978 FIFA World Cups, earning recognition as one of the finest midfielders in history. In 2004, he was honored as one of the 125 Greatest Living Footballers during a FIFA Awards Ceremony, and in 2017, he was included in the FourFourTwo list of the 100 all-time greatest players, ranking 64th.
