    Paul Breitner

    Short bio

    Paul Breitner (born September 5, 1951) is a retired German professional footballer known for his roles as a midfielder and left-back. Recognized as one of the premier players of his time, Breitner earned a place in the FIFA World Cup All-Time Team. In 2004, he was selected as one of the Top 125 greatest living footballers as part of FIFA's 100th-anniversary celebration. Breitner represented West Germany in 48 international matches and played a pivotal role in the team's victory in the 1974 FIFA World Cup, contributing a goal in the final.
