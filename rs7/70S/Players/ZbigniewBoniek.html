    Zbigniew Boniek

    Short bio

    Zbigniew Boniek (born March 3, 1956) is a retired Polish footballer and manager, currently serving as the UEFA vice-president. Once a midfielder, with versatility to play predominantly as a right winger and second striker, he is recognized as one of the most exceptional Polish players in history, earning acknowledgment from Pelé, who included him in the list of the 100 best living footballers in 2004.
