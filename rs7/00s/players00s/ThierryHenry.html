    Thierry Henry

    Short bio

    Thierry Daniel Henry, commonly known as Thierry Henry, was born on August 17, 1977, in Les Ulis, France. Renowned for his electrifying pace, exquisite skill, and clinical finishing, Henry is celebrated as one of the greatest strikers in the history of football.
