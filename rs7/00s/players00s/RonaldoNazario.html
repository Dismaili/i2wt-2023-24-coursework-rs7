    Ronaldo Nazario

    Short bio

    Ronaldo Luís Nazário de Lima, commonly known as Ronaldo, was born on September 18, 1976, in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. Renowned for his explosive pace, clinical finishing, and goal-scoring prowess, Ronaldo is hailed as one of the greatest strikers in the history of football.
