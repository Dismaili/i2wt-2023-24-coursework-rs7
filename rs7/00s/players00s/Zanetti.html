    Javier Adelmar Zanetti

    Short bio

    Javier Adelmar Zanetti, commonly known as Javier Zanetti, was born on August 10, 1973, in Buenos Aires, Argentina. Revered for his longevity, leadership, and versatility, Zanetti is regarded as one of the most iconic defenders and club captains in the history of football.
