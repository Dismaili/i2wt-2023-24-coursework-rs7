    Gianluigi Buffon

    Short bio

    Gianluigi Buffon, born on January 28, 1978, in Carrara, Italy, is a goalkeeping icon known for his remarkable shot-stopping abilities, leadership, and longevity. Buffon is widely regarded as one of the greatest goalkeepers in the history of football.

    • UEFA Club Footballer of the Year: 2003
    • Serie A Goalkeeper of the Year: Multiple occasions
    • IFFHS World's Best Goalkeeper: Multiple occasions
    • Serie A Footballer of the Year: 2006
    • Golden Foot: 2016
