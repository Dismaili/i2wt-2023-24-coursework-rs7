    Patrick Viera

    Short bio

    Patrick Vieira, born on June 23, 1976, in Dakar, Senegal, is a football legend celebrated for his imposing presence, exceptional leadership, and all-around midfield prowess. Vieira is widely regarded as one of the greatest defensive midfielders of his era.
