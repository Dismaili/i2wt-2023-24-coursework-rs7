    Roberto Carlos

    Short bio

    Roberto Carlos da Silva Rocha, commonly known as Roberto Carlos, was born on April 10, 1973, in Garça, Brazil. Renowned for his explosive speed, powerful free-kicks, and attacking prowess from the left-back position, Roberto Carlos is regarded as one of the greatest full-backs in football history.
